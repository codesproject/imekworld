ABOUT IMEK

Imekworld was established in September 2015, but became a registered entity in September 2017. We are a thriving art and beauty company with focus on giving our clients well made manicure to add colour and class to their appearance, a satisfying and painless pedicure to rock those stilettos /flat foot wear, eye lashes that sits on the eye lid to boost their self confidence and a self service kit for their DIY. Our focus is to deliver exciting services to all our customers in a premium and comfortable environment where they get the best value for their time and money.

mission

To provide exciting services to our customers in a premium and relaxed ambience, inspire our employees to keep evolving and consistently bring innovation to the industry, while adding value to the shareholders.

vision

To build a world class Art & Beauty company!
